Eighteenth Meeting of the APEC Alliance for Supply Chain Connectivity (A2C2)

August 25, 2026 · Dalian, China

Convened on the margins of APEC 2026 and supported by the U.S. Department of State through the US APEC-RISE program in collaboration with the Office of the U.S. Trade Representative.

Join customs and trade officials, private sector representatives, and supply chain experts from across APEC economies for the Eighteenth Meeting of the APEC Alliance for Supply Chain Connectivity (A2C2).

This year's meeting will examine the role of pre-arrival processing in improving trade facilitation and supply chain efficiency. Participants will explore how advance review of shipment information can reduce delays, improve predictability, and strengthen regional supply chains.

The meeting will also feature updates from leaders of other APEC groups on cross-cutting initiatives and emerging priorities affecting regional supply chains.

Register today to engage directly with APEC customs and trade officials, private sector representatives, and other stakeholders working to advance regional supply chain connectivity.

Participation Note

Participation is intended for APEC customs and trade officials, CTI and SCCP delegates, private sector representatives, and other invited stakeholders. Private sector participants requiring guest credentials should register by August 10, 2026.
